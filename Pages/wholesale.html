Wholesale Program FAQs

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our wholesale volume discount pricing begins at an MOQ of 25 units per order. You can mix colors and sizes within that product type.

Can I request pre-production physical samples?

Yes, for bulk orders exceeding 100 units, we provide physical samples for size and material approval once a deposit is cleared.

Do you ship globally?

We ship standard corporate orders across India. International bulk logistics can be arranged upon custom request.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, corporate cards, UPI, and net banking. We offer credit terms (Net 15/30) to registered long-term partners.
